Places to Visit in Porec

  • Euphrasian Basilica

    The Euphrasian Basilica is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its stunning mosaics and historic significance. It dates back to the 6th century and is a must-visit for history and architecture enthusiasts.

  • Porec Old Town

    Explore the charming streets of Porec's Old Town, filled with cobblestone alleys, medieval buildings, and vibrant cafes. It's a perfect place for a leisurely stroll and to soak in the Mediterranean atmosphere.

  • Blue Lagoon

    Take a boat trip to the Blue Lagoon, a crystal-clear bay with azure waters. It's an ideal spot for swimming, snorkeling, and enjoying the natural beauty of the Adriatic Sea.

  • Baredine Cave

    Visit the Baredine Cave, a fascinating underground world featuring unique stalactites and stalagmites. Guided tours provide insights into the cave's geological history.
